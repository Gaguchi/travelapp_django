About Us

Who we are.

We are a data science start up consisting of a team of former university and industry colleagues whose goal is to bring the latest data analytics and machine learning techniques to small businesses. Cutting edge methodologies and software are not the sole domain of big players; these technologies are now widely available for small and medium enterprises to leverage. We work with businesses to help them to take a data driven approach to their line of work. This could be as simple as developing a dashboard through to the development of machine learning algorithms. Our goal is to help you utilize data and technology to further enhance the performance of your business. We have over 50 years’ experience in this workspace between us already. We have a diverse team of data scientists, data engineers, business analysts, cyber security specialists plus website and application developers. We come with knowledge across several business domains including; healthcare, automotive, logistics, retail, food production, local government and the charity sector. Ultimately we are here to help your business generate new insights from your data and support you on the step to becoming a data driven enterprise.
