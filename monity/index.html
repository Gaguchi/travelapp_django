About Us

Who we are.

We are a data science start up consisting of a team of former university and industry colleagues whose goal is to bring the latest data analytics and machine learning techniques to small businesses.

Cutting edge methodologies and software are not the sole domain of big players; these technologies are now widely available for small and medium enterprises to leverage.

We work with businesses to help them to take an objective data driven approach to their line of work. This could be as simple as creating a dashboard through to the development of machine learning algorithms. Our goal is to help you utilize data and technology to further enhance the performance of your business.

About Us

What we do?

We solve data problems and offer business insight solutions. From data cleansing to ETL processes, dashboards to machine learning algorithms our goal is to engineer the solutions that enable your business to leverage the power of its data to give you a competitive advantage.
